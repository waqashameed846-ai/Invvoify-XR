Business Website

Transform your space virtually — view furniture and decor in real-time AR.

Project Overview

The Business Website serves as the digital presence of a company, showcasing its brand identity, services, and values to potential clients and partners. Designed with a modern and responsive layout, it enhances credibility, builds customer trust, and helps generate new business opportunities. The website acts as a central hub where users can learn about the company, explore offerings, view past projects, and easily get in touch.

Key Features

  • Responsive Design
  • About & Company Information
  • Services Section
  • Portfolio / Projects Showcase
  • Contact & Inquiry Forms
  • Blog or News Section
  • Testimonials & Reviews
  • SEO & Analytics Integration
  • Security & Performance
  • Call-to-Action (CTA) Elements

Project Details

  • Client: DecoSpace Studios
  • Category: AR / Business Website
  • Tech Stack: Unity3D, C#, ARCore, Firebase
  • Completion Date: Aprail 2025
